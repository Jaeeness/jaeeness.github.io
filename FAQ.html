    Store Information

    Q: Where is your store located?
    A: We are located at 1170 Worcester Road in Framingham, Massachusetts.
    Q: What are your store hours?
    A: We are open seven days a week. Monday through Friday we are open from 7AM-8PM. On Saturday and Sunday we are open form 7AM-5PM.
    Q: How do I get a job there?
    A: Employees do move on - so we encourage anyone interested in working at Oliver's Coffee Company to take some time and fill out our online application.
    Q: Do you recycle?
    A: Of course we do! Recycling and doing our part to help save the Earth is one of our number one goals. We break down and recycle everything we can. We cannot stand waste or overflowing trash dumps.

    Food Related Questions

    Q: Where do you buy your coffee?
    A: We our proud to have our coffee roasted and bought from Red Barn Coffee Roaster. Red Barn Coffee Roasters are from Hopkinton, Massachusetts and have been brewing coffee locally since 1997.
    Q: Who makes your food?
    A: We do! We make, bake, and serve all of our entire menu. We try our best to buy our products locally and organically.
    Q: Which coffee is your favorite?
    A: We get this question a lot. Do you know how hard it is to answer that? We are always trying new flavors, so we can't just pick one!
    Q: How long can I keep a bag of coffee before it goes stale?
    A: Usually an open bag of ground coffee last about a month before breaking down. An unopened bag of whole beans can last you around three months.

    Amenities

    Q: Do you have free Wi-Fi?
    A: Yes! We offer Wi-Fi to all of our customer at no extra charge. The Wi-Fi password is located on customer's reciept.
    Q: Do you sell merchandise?
    A: At the moment we are currently working on creating travel mugs, diner mugs, and t-shirts! We should have our merchandise available by summertime.
