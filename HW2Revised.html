𝒲elcome!

Whether you’re in the mood for a snack, a meal or just to satisfy a craving we invite you to indulge! We pride ourselves in bringing you Quality, Value, Service, and most of all, Consistency so you and your family can visit our old fashioned ice cream parlor setting and ... ENJOY!

Cabot's is a family owned and operated old-fashioned ice cream parlor and restaurant. We first opened our doors in Newton, Massachusetts in 1969! We our known for our unique premium ice cream and frozen yogurt. We serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Our food will please everyone!
